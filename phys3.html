SPEED AND ACCELERATION

So, what is speed? Well, speed is the time it takes to move from a distance to another distance. It is measured in metres per second. A Grand Prix Racing Car, for example, moves at around 100 metres per second.
You might've seen speed measured in km/h, or kilometres per hour.
