NEWTON'S LAWS

The laws of Newton are as followed: I - Every object in a state of uniform motion tends to remain in that state of motion unless an external force is applied to it. This is more commonly referred to as the "Law of Inertia".
II - The force is equal to the mass multiplied by the acceleration. The formula of this law is F = ma.
III -For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction.
