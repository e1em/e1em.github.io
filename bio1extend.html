ORGANELLES

As the previous lesson has mentioned, organelles are present in the cytoplasm of both animal and plant cells. In this lesson, you will be introduced to the basic organelles and learn what their function is.
Well, now we're going into detail and understanding some of these organelles that are present in the cytoplasm. The ones we will be studying today are called the mitochodrion, the endoplasmic reticulum, the vesicle, and the centriole.


Mitochondrion


The mitochondrion (plural: Mitonchondria) carries out respiration, an important chemical reaction that changes glucose (C6H12O6) and Oxygen into Carbon Dioxide and Water. This process is vital, as it gives animals energy.
