RASPUTIN

Rasputin, or Grigori Yefimovitch Rasputin [Григорий Ефимович Распутин] (1869-1916) was a Russian peasant and a claimed spiritual healer. He was brought in by the last Tsar of Russia, Nicholas II, to help the internal bleeding of their son. (His son had haemophilia, meaning that the blood wouldn't clot, and would bleed on until death). Apparently, he saved the kid, Alexei.
Since then, he gained lots of power, most likely because he was the lover of the Queen at the time.

Despite being uneducated and only an alleged spiritual healer, he gained lots of power during a short time, due to being considered as a womanizer and his incompetence at the job. This led to more hatred towards him (evident from the numerous assassination attempts) and the imperial family in general. Many historians believe that he was a contributing factor to the downfall of the Russian Monarchy.

He has been quite popular in modern culture, most notably the song "Rasputin" by Boney M, a German band. Below, we will analyze the lyrics.
